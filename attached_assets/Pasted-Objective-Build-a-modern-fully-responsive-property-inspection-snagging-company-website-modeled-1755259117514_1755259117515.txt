Objective

Build a modern, fully responsive property inspection & snagging company website modeled on the layout, structure, and functionality of www.urbangrid.ae, but incorporating updated services content (provided below).

The website must be SEO-optimized, ultra-fast, and mobile-first while delivering an exceptional desktop experience.

Branding & Visual Style

Primary colors: Dark green (#064E3B) & black (#000000)

Secondary: White background sections, light grey (#F5F5F5) dividers

Typography: Sleek, modern, professional, highly legible (Google Fonts recommended — e.g., Inter, Poppins, or similar)

Tone: Trustworthy, expert, friendly but authoritative

Animations: Subtle fade/slide effects for sections, cards, and on-scroll reveals (light and non-distracting)

Technology Stack

Frontend: Next.js (latest) + Tailwind CSS

CMS: Sanity (or equivalent headless CMS) for dynamic content

Performance: Optimized for SEO, Core Web Vitals, and fast load times

Hosting: Deploy-ready for Vercel or similar platform

Integrations: Email sending for forms (form submissions sent to info@urbangrid.ae)

Site Structure & Pages
1. Home Page

Hero Section:

Full-width background image/video related to Dubai/UAE real estate

Headline: "Your Trusted Property Inspection & Snagging Experts"

Subheadline: "Protecting quality, value, and your peace of mind in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah and across UAE."

Hero form (right side): Name, Email, Phone, Message → sends to info@urbangrid.ae

Services Teaser:

New build handover snagging & inspection

Post renovation / fit-out snagging inspection

Property defect liability period (DLP) snagging

Property move-in / move-out snagging

Secondary market property snagging

Developer and contractor project snagging

About Teaser – Short intro with link to full About page

Certifications/Accreditations – Logos or trust badges

Call-to-action Banners – Lead capture sections

Persistent WhatsApp & Call buttons (bottom right)

2. About Page

Company story & mission

Meet the team (with photos)

Service coverage map/areas

Trust badges/accreditations

3. Services Page

Full updated services listing (grid layout with images, titles, descriptions, and “Learn More” links)

Dedicated detail pages for each service (SEO-friendly URLs, content pulled from CMS)

4. Blog Listing Page

Paginated articles with featured images, categories, and excerpts

5. Blog Detail Page

Main article content

Social share buttons

Related posts section

Author info box

6. Contact Page

Contact form (Name, Email, Phone, Message)

Google Map embed

Office address & contact details

WhatsApp & Call buttons

Enquiry categories dropdown

7. Authentication & Admin Dashboard

Login button in site header (top-right, visible to admins only when logged in)

Authentication: Email/password login or OAuth (Google) via NextAuth.js/Auth0

Admin Dashboard:

Sidebar navigation with sections:

Dashboard Home (stats: total posts, drafts, published count)

Add New Blog (form with title, slug auto-generator, category selector, tags, image upload, WYSIWYG editor for content)

Manage Blogs (list with edit/delete options)

Profile Settings

Role-based access (only admins can create/edit/delete blogs)

8. Blog Management System

Blog data stored in database with fields:

id, title, slug, featured_image, category, tags, excerpt, content, author_id, status (draft/published), created_at, updated_at

SEO-friendly URLs (/blog/[slug])

Support for rich text editor (images, headings, links, bullet points)

Blog listing auto-updates when new post is published

Header & Footer

Header: Logo left, navigation center, sticky with transparent-to-solid scroll effect

Footer: Quick links, contact info, copyright

CTAs & Forms

Floating WhatsApp button (bottom-right, site-wide)

Floating Call button (adjacent to WhatsApp button)

All forms send submissions to info@urbangrid.ae

Mobile-first responsive design
